Seven Signature Programs

Pathways to Innovation & Opportunity

Every program is rooted in youth voice, community need, and proven impact. We combine creativity, technology, and entrepreneurship to build dignified futures for displaced and underserved youth in Kakuma.

Program 1

AvanTech: Digital Innovation

Technology, AI, Robotics & Coding

Comprehensive STEM education empowering youth to become digital innovators. From robotics and coding to AI and cybersecurity, AvanTech builds foundational and advanced technical skills with real-world applications.

What's Included:

  • ✓ Robotics & mechanical engineering (LEGO, Arduino)
  • ✓ Web and mobile app development
  • ✓ AI fundamentals and machine learning basics
  • ✓ Cybersecurity and digital safety
  • ✓ Freelancing platforms and digital entrepreneurship
  • ✓ Hackathons and innovation competitions

Impact

Participants gain access to in-demand digital economy skills, create income opportunities through freelancing, and position themselves as innovators solving community problems.

Program 4

I Am Art: Visual & Creative Expression

Painting, Sculpture, Digital Art & More

Dedicated visual arts program exploring drawing, painting, sculpture, photography, digital art, murals, and community art projects. A space for emotional expression, healing, and creative development.

What's Included:

  • ✓ Drawing, painting, and sculpture techniques
  • ✓ Photography and digital art
  • ✓ Mural and community public art
  • ✓ Art exhibitions and showcases
  • ✓ Art-based therapy and healing
  • ✓ Gallery representation and artist development

Impact

Youth process trauma through creative expression, build confidence and self-worth, develop professional art practices, and contribute to community cultural vibrancy.

Other Signature Programs

Six More Pathways to Innovation

Pekee Models: Professional Performance

Runway Training & Talent Development

Professional modeling and performance program focused on runway training, confidence building, personal branding, and talent development.

Miradede: Fashion & Design

Garment Making & Sustainable Fashion

Creative program focused on fashion design, garment production, tailoring, and sustainable fashion innovation. From design to production.

Dance

Training, Choreography & Performance

After our performers stole the show at our June Pride event, we're formalizing Dance as its own program pillar with dedicated training time.

Music Studio

Production, Songwriting & Recording

Fully equipped music studio so our musicians can turn live performance into lasting recorded material and income opportunities.

Storytelling & Podcast

Narrative, Audio, & Oral History

Dedicated podcast room where writers, storytellers, and podcasters can record and share their stories in audio form to audiences beyond Kakuma.

Entrepreneurship & Livelihoods

Business Skills & Income Generation

Cross-cutting program that helps youth turn their creative and technical skills into sustainable income and livelihood opportunities.
