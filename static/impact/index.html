Success Stories

From Participant to Mentor

Ahmad attended our Digital Skills program two years ago. Today, he mentors new participants and has launched his own web design freelance business, earning income for his family while supporting his community.

— Ahmad, 19, Web Developer

Creative Expression & Healing

Through our Creative Arts Lab, Amina discovered her passion for video production. Her documentary about refugee resilience has been screened locally and has become a powerful voice for her community.

— Amina, 17, Filmmaker

Entrepreneurship & Independence

Fatima completed our Entrepreneurship Bootcamp and launched a small crafts business selling handmade items online. She now employs three other youth from her camp and earns sustainable income.

— Fatima, 20, Social Entrepreneur
