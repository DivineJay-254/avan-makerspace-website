Our Mission

At Avan Makerspace, we believe every young person deserves the opportunity to discover their potential. Operating in Kakuma Refugee Camp, we provide a safe, affirming space where displaced youth can develop essential digital and creative skills, explore entrepreneurship, and build a sustainable future.

As a queer-led and refugee-led organization, we center the voices and experiences of those most marginalized, creating inclusive programs that celebrate diversity and foster community.
